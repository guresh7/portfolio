-- About the artist

Hi, I'm Guruprasad

I'm a self-taught artist based in India, working primarily in pencil
and charcoal portraiture. My passion is turning ordinary
photographs into timeless sketches that capture emotion,
not just likeness. Every piece is hand drawn, no shortcuts,
no filters — just hours of focus and a steady hand

What started as a hobby in school has grown into a small
commission practice, people turn their favourite
memories into framed art they'll treasure for years.
